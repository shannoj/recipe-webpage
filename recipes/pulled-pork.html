Best Pulled Pork

Pulled pork is a delicous meal that can be enjoyed in many different ways. For example, you can put it on tacos or on a nice brioche for a pulled pork sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Onions
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Orange

Steps

  1. Place pork in slow cooker
  2. Cover with cumin, oregano, orange juice, and diced onions
  3. Cook on slow for 10 hours
